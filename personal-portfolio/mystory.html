my story

I combine two worlds

I combine two worlds – a classic craft workshop and a dynamic digital technology space. I am a person who highly values precision, structure and beauty in both the physical and digital worlds.

My path began in Kurzeme

My path began in Kurzeme, Latvia – I was born and raised in Saldus. The place where my first creative sense and interest in aesthetics, proportions and the beauty of details developed. Then the twists and turns of life took me to Riga. Here I specialized in modeling style furniture, later becoming an experienced antique furniture restorer who carefully restored historical interior masterpieces for 13 years.

Furniture restoration – more than a profession

Furniture restoration has always been more than a profession for me – it was a form that combines respect for the past with an artistic vision for a sustainable future. As a senior master, I have participated in complex restoration projects, developing patience, care and the ability to work with unique, often unrepeatable materials and designs.

Change of direction

However, times change – and so do we. I have always been fascinated by the world of technology, the possibilities it offers and the logical structures it creates. That is why I have decided to radically change my professional direction and have started studying IT.

Passions beyond work

In my free time, I devote myself to photography and video production, which allows me to maintain an aesthetic perspective outside of professional life. Travel inspires me – getting to know new cultures, architecture and visual languages. I am also interested in digital culture trends and visual storytelling on social media.

My current goal

I am technically curious, with a strong visual sense and the ability to see potential in both old furniture and new programs. My current goal is to establish myself in the IT field, where creativity, structure and digital precision become a unified force.
